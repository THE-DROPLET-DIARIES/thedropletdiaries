About Us

This website was born out of extreme rage for the mismanagement of the COVID-19 epidemic in the United States and continued inequality in vaccine distribution worldwide. Our primary purpose is to avoid infection or reinfection with COVID-19 and help others achieve the same.

Welcome to The Droplet Diaries

THIS IS WHO WE ARE

This website is here to clear the air on the illness COVID-19 caused by the virus SARS2. Our primary purpose is to avoid infection and reinfection with COVID-19 and help others achieve the same.

We are alarmed by long COVID

We are alarmed by the long-term impacts of reinfections, breakthrough, and “mild,” infections.

We reject herd immunity

We reject any infection-based strategies that attempt to achieve herd immunity for the virus SARS-CoV2.

We promote SARS2 control

We want vaccine equity, epidemiological control measures, and airborne precautions against an airborne virus.
